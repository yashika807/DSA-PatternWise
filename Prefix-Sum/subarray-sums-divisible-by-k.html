Subarray Sums Divisible by K

Walk the array keeping a running balance (prefixSum). At every step, work out which of the k clock slots (prefixSum % k, normalized) it lands on, and ask the ledger: "have I landed on this exact slot before?" Every past day on the same slot means a subarray ending here is divisible by k. Record today's slot in the ledger and move on.

Time  O(n) Space  O(min(n,k)) Pattern  Prefix Sum + HashMap
