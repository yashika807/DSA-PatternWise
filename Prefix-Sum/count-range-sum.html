Count of Range Sum

Walk the array keeping a running balance (prefixSum). At every step, ask the sorted shelf of past balances: "how many of you fall inside [prefixSum − upper, prefixSum − lower]?" — a whole range, not one exact value. A real Fenwick Tree answers this in O(log n) via coordinate compression; here we show the equivalent range-count directly. Insert today's balance and move on.

Time  O(n log n) Space  O(n) Pattern  Prefix Sum + Fenwick Tree
