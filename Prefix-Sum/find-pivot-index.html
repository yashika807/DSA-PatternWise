Find Pivot Index

Precompute the whole array's totalSum once. Then walk left → right keeping a running leftSum. At every index, rightSum falls out of pure arithmetic (totalSum − leftSum − nums[i]) — no re-scanning, no HashMap. The moment leftSum == rightSum, that's your pivot.

Time  O(n) Space  O(1) Pattern  Prefix Sum
