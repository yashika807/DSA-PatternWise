Minimum Days for m Bouquets

Another "search over the answer", not the array. We binary search the calendar day (min(bloomDay) to max(bloomDay)). Each candidate day is tested with a feasibility check: scan the garden left-to-right, count consecutive already-bloomed flowers, and every run of k forms a bouquet. Enough bouquets? Try an earlier day. Not enough? Wait longer.
