Rotate Image 90°

LeetCode 48. Rotate an n×n matrix clockwise in place — no second matrix. The trick: transpose across the diagonal, then reverse each row. Each cell is tinted by its original position, so you can watch the whole "image" turn a quarter-turn.

Time O(n²) Space O(1) Difficulty Medium
