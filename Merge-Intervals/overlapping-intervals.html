Any Two Intervals Overlap?

Sort the bookings by start time. A neat mathematical fact then kicks in: if any two bookings overlap anywhere in the list, the two bookings sitting right next to each other in sorted order must overlap too. So just walk the sorted list and check each adjacent pair — the moment one overlaps, you have your answer.
