Interval List Intersections

Two employees, two sorted busy-schedules. Walk pointer i through Schedule A and pointer j through Schedule B. At every step, take the overlap of the two current meetings — whoever's meeting ends first steps to their next meeting, since that's the only one that can still produce a new overlap.
