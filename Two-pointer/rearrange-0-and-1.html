Segregate 0s and 1s

A laundry basket with two sections. The Low boundary (low) marks where the next 0 must land. The Scanner (i) walks the whole array once. Find a 0? Swap it into the low-section, both pointers move. Find a 1? It's already home — only the Scanner moves. Step through and watch it play out.
