Middle of the Linked List

List ki length pehle se pata nahi — ek pass mein hi middle node dhoondna hai. Tortoise (slow, +1) aur hare (fast, +2) dono head se chalte hain. Jab hare list khatam kar de (finish line pe pahunche), tortoise theek beech mein khada hoga — kyunki hare hamesha tortoise se double speed se chalta hai.
