Happy Number

Koi fixed array ya linked list nahi hai yahan — har number apne digits ke squares ka sum nikal ke agla number "point" karta hai (jaise 19 → 1²+9² = 82). Yeh khud ek virtual chain ban jaati hai. Chain ya to 1 par jaake khud se loop karti hai (happy), ya famous 8-number cycle (4→16→37→58→89→145→42→20→4) mein phas jaati hai (unhappy). Slow & fast pointer chala ke cycle pakdo, phir check karo 1 mila ya nahi.
